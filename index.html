About the cauldron

Cozy spellbook, not haunted house.

Hexpot&Co is a small disabled-run craft brand making wearable charms for everyday life: press-on nails, knitted hats, and themed gift sets that feel a little bit magic.

The brand guide defines the tone as warm, punny, witchy-cute, and honest — more curiosity shop above an indie bookstore than faux-mystical luxury branding.

The three brews

01

Cuticle Coven

Hand-painted press-on nails, themed drops, and custom commissions with soft magic and practical wearability.

02

Hat Trick Hexes

Hand-knit hats in soft pastels and moody darks, made to order so nothing sits around waiting to become waste.

03

Brew Boxes

Giftable bundles for birthdays, friend dates, comfort mail, and seasonal little treats.

Voice

Warm, punny, and a little bit weird.

The guide recommends copy that feels friendly and specific: “Brewed in small batches,” “A tiny spell for your hands,” and “Tucked into the Brew Box this month…”

Made-to-order, made-to-last, made by a disabled human.

Transparency is part of the brand. Hexpot&Co is built around community, care, and sustainability-minded small-batch making rather than overproduction.
