Tips for press-on longevity
Prep your nails carefully (push back cuticles, file nailbeds, clean nailbeds).
Apply glue to both the press-on and your natural nail. (For maximum longevity, I personally like to use both glue *and* sticky tabs.)
Don't get your hands wet for at least 2 hours after application. (I like to do mine right before bed.)
Wear gloves when doing housework, and don't use your press-ons as tools (no matter how tempting). It's amazing how many things you can do with the sides if your fingers instead of your fingertips.
