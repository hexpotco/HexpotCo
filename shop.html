A note on making

Made-to-order, made-to-last, made by a disabled human.

Hexpot&Co is built around community, care, and sustainability-minded small-batch making rather than overproduction. This means you'll get a custom experience from start to finish, and creation time reflects the lived reality of a chronically ill creator.
