About us

Hexpot & Co. is a small, disabled-run craft brand making wearable charms for everyday life — press-on nails, knitted hats, and themed gift sets that feel a little bit magic. Every piece is hand-made or hand-gathered in small batches.

Think cozy spellbook, not haunted house. Cartoon cauldron, not crystal ball. We're celestial, witchy-cute, and a little nerdy — built around community, not vibes-only consumerism. Slow magic over fast fashion.

Hexpot exists because making things by hand — slowly, with care, in small batches — is itself a kind of spell. We make for people who want their everyday objects to feel a little enchanted, and who'd rather buy from a disabled human than a mega-retailer.

Meet the Owner

Nerdy, colorful, and a little bit weird.

Null is a mid-30s witchy woman living in Mississippi with her partner and their doggo. She is mentally and chronically ill, which makes the accessibility aspect of this business a personal investment as well as ethical obligation. An education in Philosophy and English lit, paired with illness, has unsurprisingly led to struggling to survive in the current economy. Hexpot&Co is her attempt at earning a livelihood without wrecking her crumbling body anymore than absolutely necessary.

💅Q: What are your queer accessibility efforts?

💅A: In recognition that not every fabulous femme and tantalizing them in our community can casually wear nails out, all of our press-on sets come with the option to add on the sticky tab application method at no cost.
This lets you wear sets for just a few hours or possibly the whole weekend, if applied well and worn with care, and all of our items come discreetly packaged.

In a nice little example of universal design benefiting more than just marginalized communities, this is also great for nurses and people in the food industry, as well as people who just want press-ons for a few hours for photos or an event.


💅Q: What are your general accessibility efforts?

💅A: I intend to put a detailed image description on each of my photos.
I offer a patient, custom order experience that I hope to make as inclusive and accessible as possible.
I try to send everything you'll need for your press-on experience, to maximum ensure financial accessibility.
I offer "sensory friendly" orders (absolutely no decor on the nails for the smoothest experience, all unscented items in Brew Boxes).
I offer "fidget nail" orders (gems and tactile decor on every nail, maximizing for tactile interest).


💅Q: How long do your press-ons typically last?

💅A: With glue and good prep, many people get about 4-14 days of wear depending on how gentle they are with their hands. With sticky tabs, it’s more like a few hours to a couple of days, depending on how gentle you are with your hands.


💅Q: Can I reuse my press-ons?

💅A: Yes, as long as you remove them gently and clean off leftover glue or tabs, most sets can be reused multiple times. This is easiest with sticky tabs, glue may need to be filed off.


💅Q: Do you ship discreetly?

💅A: Yes. All items are sent in discreet packaging with no obvious branding that screams “beauty products” on the outside.


💅Q: Do you take rush orders?

A: Sometimes! If you have a specific date or event, message me first so I can confirm whether I can meet your timeline and whether a rush fee will apply.


💅Q: Do you offer refunds or replacements if something is wrong with my order?

💅A: I do not offer refunds or replacements for orders lost in the mail, but if your order arrives and there's a problem with sizing or quality, please message me and I will do my best to fix any problems.
If your order arrives unwearably damaged by the mail, message me within 48 hours of delivery confirmation with proof of damage in order to receive a full replacement at no cost to yourself.


💅Q: Do you ship outside the U.S.?

A: Currently standard shipping is US-only, but feel free to message me if you’re elsewhere and I can look into specific policies and prices.


💅Q: I have large nail beds, will your press-on products be accessible to me?

💅A: Probably! Sizing is done very early in the design process, so there’s very little risk of a set being made and mailed that ends up too small. You’re welcome to DM for a free online sizing consultation (using the quarter method), or order a Sizing Kit (includes every size of specified shapes; you then size your nails and send the information to me).


💅Q: What payment methods do you accept?

💅A: Along with the Ko-fi shop; currently I typically accept Cashapp, Paypal, and Venmo. Cash payment is available for local orders.
